About Me

I have loved aspects of all my previous roles and am now eager to find something that “has it all”. Since the pandemic, WFH has meant sharing a workspace with a software engineer, and this has given me an invaluable insight into the profession and its processes - stand-ups, sprints, retros etc. As someone who is motivated by effective collaboration, high performance, continuous learning and intellectual challenge, the field very much appeals.

As a colleague, I'm adept at asking the right questions and at thinking ahead to forestall problems. Being a linguist, I'm a clear, adaptable communicator. I am looking forward to securing a position where I will be supported to understand my new craft inside out, and to constantly improve.
